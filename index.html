The benefits of handmade soap

Our soaps are made with organic soap bases, vegetable oils and natural purified fats. It also contains alkalis, essential oils and natural dyes.

The advantage of using handmade soap is the inclusion of therapeutic ingredients that multiply its benefits for the skin. Examples of such components are various valuable plant oils and plant extracts.Therefore, such a cosmetic product is harmless to the skin and suitable for children.
